About the Team

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is one of IPL's most successful franchises, led by the legendary MS Dhoni. Known for their strong fan base, consistent performance, and thrilling cricket since 2008.

CSK has won the IPL title 4 times (2010, 2011, 2018, 2023) and is famous for its never-say-die attitude.
